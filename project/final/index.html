Smite

Learn more about this game here.

Released on March 25, 2014

Based around mythology from across the world, players work with their team to destroy the enemy towers and phoenixes to bring down the Titan, before the enemy team does so.

About this game

In Smite, players control a god, goddess or other mythological figure and take part in team-based combat, using their abilities and tactics against other player-controlled gods and non-player-controlled minions.
